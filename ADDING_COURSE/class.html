Lesson 1: Introduction to Photosynthesis

Understanding how plants convert light into energy

Lesson Text

Photosynthesis is the process by which plants use sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide to create oxygen and energy in the form of sugar.

The process takes place in the chloroplasts of plant cells, where chlorophyll captures light energy and drives the reactions needed for plant growth.
